## 8) ROI Framing (For Client Discussions)

Use value-based framing in commercial conversations:

- If time savings are approximately 5 minutes per case across ~3,000 surgeries/year, annual recovered OR time is ~15,000 minutes.
- At typical OR value assumptions, this often supports **six-figure to low seven-figure annual operational value**.
- Pricing is intentionally set to represent a fraction of measurable value while preserving budget predictability.
